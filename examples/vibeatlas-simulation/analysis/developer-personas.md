# VibeAtlas User Personas

## Product Overview

VibeAtlas is a mood tracking and social discovery app that helps users understand their emotional patterns and connect with others on similar journeys.

## Target Audience

Primary: Young adults (18-35) interested in mental wellness, self-improvement, and social connection.

## Key Personas

### 1. The Mental Health Tracker (30%)
- Uses app daily for mental health management
- Privacy-conscious, prefers private/friends-only sharing
- Values accuracy and insights over social features
- Likely in therapy or considering it

### 2. The Social Sharer (25%)
- Excited to share mood journey publicly
- Early adopter, active on social media
- Values community and connection
- Drives viral growth through sharing

### 3. The Curious Explorer (20%)
- Trying app to understand themselves better
- Uses intermittently, not daily habit yet
- Interested in AI insights
- Needs strong value to stick around

### 4. The Mood Twin Seeker (15%)
- Primarily interested in finding similar people
- Values discovery features over tracking
- Medium privacy (friends or selected public)
- Strong engagement when connections found

### 5. The Skeptical Data Tracker (10%)
- Wants quantified self-data
- Skeptical of AI insights
- Needs export and offline access
- Power user if features meet needs
